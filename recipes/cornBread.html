Corn Bread Recipe

This cornbread recipe goes well with just about anything. I always make it when we have chili. The sour cream makes it nice and moist.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1 cup cornmeal
  • 1/4 cup white sugar
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoond baking soda
  • 1 egg, lightly beaten
  • 1 cup sour cream
  • 1/3 cup milk
  • 1/4 cup butter, melter

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 400 degress F. Lightly grease an 8 inch square baking dish
  2. Combine flour, cornmeal, sugar, baking powder, baking soda, and salt in a large bowl. Mix egg, sour cream, milk, and butter in a small bowl.
    Fold egg mixture into flour mixture until jsut moistened; pour into prepared baking dish.
  3. Bake in a preheated overn until a toothpick inserted near the center comes out clean, 20 to 25 minutes. Serve warm.
